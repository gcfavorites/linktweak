"Rewrite links matched with:" and "Rewrite pattern" are regular expressions. For example:
http://code.google.com/apis/… ⇒ http://code.google.com/intl/en/apis/…
Rewrite links matched with:^http://code\.google\.com/apis/
Rewrite pattern:/apis/
Substitution:/intl/en/apis/
http://maps.google.com/maps?hl=en… ⇒ http://maps.google.com/maps?hl=ja…
Rewrite links matched with:^http://maps\.google\.co\.jp/maps
Rewrite pattern:([?&])hl=en
Substitution:$1hl=ja
